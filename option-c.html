Protect Archiving: Off-Site Backup That Works

This is why Option C uses UniFi Protect instead of Hikvision. Native off-site backup without the pain.

What It Does

  • Critical footage archived to Whitney NOC NAS
  • Event-based (motion alerts, specific timeframes)
  • Selected cameras only (tills, entrances, stock rooms)
  • Automatic, no manual intervention

Why It Matters

  • Fire at site → footage safe at Whitney
  • Theft/break-in → evidence preserved
  • Equipment failure → backup exists
  • Insurance-grade evidence retention

UniFi Protect Advantage

  • Native integration with network infrastructure
  • Single ecosystem for cameras, recording, management
  • Seamless archiving configuration
  • Reliable sync between sites and NOC
